Former Board Members

  • Chris MacDougald - San Antonio - Founder
    After attending BrickFest in 2003, Chris worked to grow the LEGO community in Texas to support a convention type event. After, growing the San Antonio area and doing lots of public events. Chris connected with Kurt when the Barton Creek store opened in Austin and they went to talk to Omni Hotel in Austin for the inaugural event. Chris remained on the board until resigning in Janurary 2017.
